SMART HOME GARDEN



With the increasing concern of global warming, there has been a greater onus on people to be part of the solution by growing their own plants. Sustainable gardening and landscaping techniques can slow future warming by reducing carbon emissions. However, plants need continuous and closely monitored care.



As a solution, we propose an IoT based home garden system that continuously monitors the conditions of plants and soil, so the plants could be provided water and shade as required.
